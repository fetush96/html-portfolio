The Best Books According to Oscar

My top 3 books of all-time

The Second Life Of Uwe, Fredrik Bachman

We all got a little bit of Uwe's attitude. It's a good book to reconsider your way of thinking and start living your life differently. The book is a good read for anyone.

Educated, Tara Westover

The autobiographical novel narrating the wildest experience of the author and all the things she was able to achieve regardless. The book made me feel a wide range of emotions and was a food for thought. It also kept me very involved during that couple of days when I was reading it.

11/22/63, Stephen King

This is a very exciting and special story. Loved every part of it.
